Activitylighter

ActivityLight is a Luxafor status program that reads your availability from your Lync client or Exchange account and sets the color of the Luxafor light thereby. You can also choose your own availability and ActivityLighter will update your Lync status.

Availability USB light devices

If you have any other USB availability devices other than Luxafor(r) that could be integrated, please let me know.
